My dissertation focuses on the application of AI in analyzing criminal behaviors. Specifically, focusing on how the integration of crime theories, network science, NLP and machine learning techniques can be applied to highlight terrorists use of technologies.

Tools and techniques include statistical and computational techniques such as social network analysis, NLP/sentiment analysis, machine learning predictive models and qualitative methods for data analysis.
